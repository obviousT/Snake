Snake Game with Difficulty Levels and Enhanced Features

This project is a modern take on the classic Snake game, implemented in JavaScript and HTML5. The game includes various features and enhancements to provide a more engaging and dynamic gameplay experience.

Features:-

Difficulty Levels:
Players can select the difficulty level using a slider. The difficulty level affects the speed of the snake, making the game more challenging as the level increases.

Dynamic Speed Adjustment:
The speed of the game dynamically adjusts based on the selected difficulty level. This allows for a customizable and scalable challenge, ensuring that both beginners and experienced players can enjoy the game.

Pause Functionality:
The game can be paused and resumed using the spacebar. When paused, the game and background music stop, allowing players to take breaks without losing progress.

High Score Tracking:
The game tracks the highest score achieved during play and saves it in the browser's local storage. This high score is displayed on the screen and is updated whenever a new high score is achieved.

Responsive Design:
The game board and elements are designed to be responsive, ensuring a smooth experience across different devices and screen sizes.

Sound Effects and Background Music:
The game includes sound effects for actions such as eating food and game over. Additionally, there is background music that loops during gameplay, enhancing the overall experience.

Food Placement Logic:
The food is placed randomly on the board, but never on the snake's body. This ensures that the game remains fair and challenging.

How to Play

Starting the Game:
Begin on the home page where you can start the game or navigate to the difficulty selection page.

Selecting Difficulty:
On the difficulty selection page, use the slider to choose a difficulty level from 1 to 10. Click the "Set Difficulty" button to save your selection and start the game.

Gameplay:
Use the arrow keys to control the direction of the snake. Eat the red food blocks to grow the snake and increase your score. Avoid running into the walls or the snake's own body.

Pausing the Game:
Press the spacebar to pause or resume the game.

Game Over:
If the snake runs into the wall or its own body, the game ends. Click "OK" on the alert to be redirected back to the home page.
